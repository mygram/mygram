What is Mygram?

Rich notifications over SMS

Mygram is an SMS based notification service that enables you to send notifications to your users by email - We'll send them a link to that email by SMS

Dealing with SMS gateways can be frustrating, complex and expensive. With Mygram, onboarding and notifications are handled over SMS through a simple mechanism - your user just SMS's your three-letter code to 56677 to be subscribed to your service, and can thereafter be notified by simply sending an email to <user's phone number>@mygram.me.

How it works

User SMS's <YOUR CODE> to 56677
(Try 'MGM' for a demo)
User receives an activation URL and
clicks it to activate his/her account
You can now email notifications to
<user's mobile number>@mygram.me
